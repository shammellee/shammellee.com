Benefits and Tools

Get the Card that comes with business-sized buying power

The Business Gold Rewards Card from American Express OPEN isn't made for little expenses. It's made for running your business. You could book company travel, pay your fleet's gas, outfit your office with equipment, and advertise your business. The Card
gives you spending power that can grow as your business grows. The more you use
your Card to run your business, the more points you can earn.

ReceiptMatchSM with QuickBooks®4

American Express OPEN offers the only Business Cards that directly integrate
with Intuit QuickBooks.

Here's how it works:

  • Snap: Take a picture of your receipt and ReceiptMatch will match and
    attach it to your Card transaction.
  • Tag: Label the transaction with your own QuickBooks category, right from
    your American Express online statement of ReceiptMatch mobile app.
  • Transfer To QuickBooks: Review and send the transaction data with
    a link to the receipt image directly to your QuickBooks company file.
  • Done: You don't have to enter transaction data manually into
    QuickBooks—your expense is now filed.
